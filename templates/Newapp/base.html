About Us

Kritagya is a cultural fiesta to celebrate a profession that forges all other professionals. This occasion is an initiative by the students of School of Computer Engineering to pay tribute and gratitude to our teachers for their continuous, selfless and precious efforts in shaping our future.

A Saying

A very famous quote says, "The collapse of education is the collapse of the Nation." Our teachers are the ones who enrich all the quality education system in the country and process it continuously without getting tired. They prepare us to tackle any situation of life through knowledge and patience.
